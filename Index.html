About Us

Located in Uttar Matlab, Chandpur, our resort spans over 40 bighas of private land, with additional government land allocated for green development. Enjoy nature, spacious rooms, and a serene getaway by the river.

Our Accommodation

We offer 20+ spacious rooms, each with attached bathrooms. The resort features large lobbies on the 2nd and 3rd floors, and a private glasshouse on the 4th floor for an exclusive experience.
